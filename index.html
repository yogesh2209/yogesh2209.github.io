Autonomous RC Car

Big data and Computer vision application

Objective

The RC car will be modified in order to drive on its own with the help of a camera that will detect lanes and recognize objects on its path. The car will also have the capabilities of stopping before it crashes since it has a distance sensor on the front bumper.

Hardware

  • Any cheap RC Car will do
  • Raspberry Pi 3
  • Pi Camera
  • HC-SR04 Distance Sensor
  • L298N motor driver (H-Bridge)
  • Edimax wifi adapter
  • Power bank (power source)
  • Resistors and jumper wires

Software

  • Python 3.6
  • OpenCV
  • Framework/Libraries: Numpy, Scipy, PiSerial, Pandas, and many more

Resources

  • Rapid object detection using a boosted cascade of simple features ( Viola and Jones 2001)
  • Haar Cascade Explained
